Here are some ways climate change is affecting us.

Climate Change threatens plants and animals, food supply, clean air and water. Climate Change, as you might have guessed, is not a joke. We have to do our best to stop it because if we don't, it will be bad for not only animals and plants, but for us.


Animals

More that a million animals are at risk from climate change, and could go extinct. This is not good, as we are the ones causing this. Scientists predict that by mid-century, 30 to 50% of all animals on earth will have gone extinct.


Can we reverse our doings?

By the way we are going, Climate Change is not stopping at all. By the year 2030, scientists say that some effects of Climate Change are going to be irreversible.


Extreme weather

The worst climate change gets, the more extreme weather we will get. This includes frequent tornadoes, hurricanes, and forest fires. There will also be drier weather for some countries, heavier snow for others.


Rising sea levels

Sea levels are rising, and it is due to climate change. What does climate change have to do with anything with the sea? When the climate gets warmer near the glaciers, the ice from the glaciers melts into the sea. This is harming polar bears, because they need ice blocks to survive, and it also harms our city. With rising water, by 2050, a lot of places will be drowned by the water.
